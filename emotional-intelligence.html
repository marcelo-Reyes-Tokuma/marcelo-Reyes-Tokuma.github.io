    • What is emotional Intelligence? - 'The ability to monitor one's own and other's feelings and emotions, to discriminate among them and to use this information to guides one's thinking and actions' -Salvoey and Mayer 1990


    How is EQ different to IQ? - While IQ is in relation to your logical reasoning, problem solving and Analytical skills, EQ refers to your aptitude for understanding and managing emotions, empathising with others and building effective interpersonal relationships.


    Why is EQ important? - The three primary benefits of strengthening EQ: Stellar Performance, Outstanding Leadership and Happiness.
